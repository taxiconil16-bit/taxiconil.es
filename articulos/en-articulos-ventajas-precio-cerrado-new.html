It is not an estimate or an approximate price that can vary upon reaching the destination. It is a commitment to absolute transparency. When you make your booking through our web calculator or via WhatsApp, the amount you receive will be the final and total price of your journey.

Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly does the fixed price include?
It includes the entire journey without additional costs: driver, vehicle, insurance, luggage and any applicable taxes. No surprises or extra charges.
Is it more expensive than the meter?
On long routes or with traffic it is usually more economical. Plus it offers the peace of mind of knowing the exact cost from the start.
What happens if there is traffic or detours?
The price does not change. We assume the risk of delays due to traffic, roadworks or detours. You always pay what was agreed.
Can I cancel without cost?
We do not charge anything for cancellation. You can cancel your service at any time without additional costs or management fees.
