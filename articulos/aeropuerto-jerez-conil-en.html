From Conil to Jerez Airport: Transport Guide (XRY)

Jerez Airport (XRY) is the closest and most convenient connection for those visiting the Costa de la Luz. Located about 75 kilometres from Conil, booking a private transfer in advance is the most reliable way to ensure your punctual arrival. Here we tell you how to optimise your airport transfer.

Journey Conil – Jerez Airport

The estimated road travel time is about 50 minutes via the A-48 motorway and the AP-4. Being a relatively short journey, it is the preferred option for most travellers, but not without planning, especially in high season when traffic towards airports intensifies.

If you are considering going by bus or combining with train, keep in mind that from Conil you will normally have to connect with another town, and that means transfers, limited schedules and less buffer for delays. With luggage and a boarding time, the "risk" usually lies in the connection: if you miss one, making up the time is difficult.

2. Step-by-step guide for your departure

To ensure your return journey is as relaxed as your stay, follow these recommendations:

Prior Booking

Secure your vehicle by booking at least 24 hours in advance, especially on weekends.

Group Travel

If you are travelling with friends or a large family, we have vehicles with greater passenger capacity.

Documentation

Keep your tickets and ID/Passport handy. A direct transfer allows you to review everything calmly.

Online Check-in

Save time and travel light by having your boarding pass ready on your mobile before pick-up.
