Travelling with Children: Safety and Comfort in your transfers

Back to Blog

For families visiting the Costa de la Luz, safety is the absolute priority. At Taxi Conil 24h, we offer a comprehensive family transfer service with approved child seats, so that your transfer with children is as safe as it is comfortable.

1. Regulations and Child Restraint Systems (CRS)

On intercity journeys, the use of approved child seats is not just an option, it is a legal and ethical obligation. We have a fleet of restraint systems that comply with the most demanding European regulations (i-Size), adapting to the growth of your children:

Group 0 and 0+

Maxi-Cosi and carrycots designed for babies, guaranteeing the correct ergonomic position and maximum cervical protection.

Group 1 and 2

Seats with five-point harnesses, robust and comfortable for children who are already starting to explore the world from the window.

Group 3 (Boosters)

Boosters with and without backrests to ensure that the seat belt works correctly for older children.

Approved CRS Seats

Our entire fleet features child restraint systems compliant with European i-Size regulations, ensuring maximum safety and peace of mind for your family.

Professional equipment: CRS seats ready for every stage of growth.

To ensure we provide the specific seat your child requires, please specify their age or approximate weight when making your booking.

2. Vehicles Adapted to Family Life

We know that travelling with children involves a lot of luggage: strollers, travel cots, toy bags, and large suitcases. Our fleet is not only safe, but also practical:

XL Trunks

Enough capacity for two strollers and complete luggage without sacrificing passenger space.

Dual Climate Control

Independent temperature control so that children always travel cool and comfortable.

3. Tips for a Perfect Family Trip

As experts in the Conil area, these are our tips for making the journey part of the holiday:

  • Schedule planning: If travelling with babies, choosing nap times for the transfer can make the trip much calmer.
  • Onboard entertainment: Our drivers are happy to adjust the music or volume so that the little ones travel entertained.
  • Loading assistance: Let us take care of placing the suitcases and the stroller; you just make sure the children get in safely.

Looking for a safe transfer service?

Our private transfer service is designed to protect your most precious cargo: your children. We understand that safety is your top priority when travelling with family.

Frequently Asked Questions

What type of child seats do you offer?
We have approved seats for all ages. Just indicate this when confirming your booking so everything is ready for your arrival.
Are the child seats free of charge?
Yes, all child seats are included at no additional cost. We provide appropriate seats for every age group.
Do your drivers have safety training?
Yes, all our drivers receive specialized training in child safety and correct installation of car seats.
Can I bring my own child seat?
Yes, if you prefer, you can bring your own seat. We will ensure it is properly installed according to manufacturer specifications.
What happens if my flight is delayed?
Don't worry. We monitor all flights in real time and adjust pickup time without additional cost. Your driver will be waiting.
