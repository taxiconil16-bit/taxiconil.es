Frequently asked questions

How do I book my journey?
Use our online calculator to obtain an estimated fare, complete your details and you will receive an instant confirmation. You may also contact us via WhatsApp.
What happens if my flight is delayed?
We monitor flights in real time. If your flight is delayed, your driver will wait for you at the terminal at no additional cost during the courtesy waiting time. Your peace of mind is our priority.
Can I pay by card in Conil de la Frontera taxis?
Yes. Taxi Conil accepts card payments (Visa, Mastercard, Apple Pay and Google Pay), Bizum and cash at the end of the journey. The driver carries a card terminal in the vehicle.
Do you offer child seats?
Yes, we have approved seats for all ages. You just need to indicate it when making your booking so that we have it installed at no additional cost.
Are prices per person or per vehicle?
Our prices are per vehicle (up to 4 passengers), not per person. This makes us a very competitive option for families and small groups.
Where does the driver wait for me at the airport?
Your driver will wait for you in the arrivals hall, holding a sign with your name or the Taxi Conil logo for a quick and professional pick-up.
Do you do transfers to other points in Spain?
Yes, we offer transfers to destinations across mainland Spain. If your destination is not listed, use our calculator to get an instant quote.
Is it necessary to book far in advance?
We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance to guarantee availability, especially in high season, although we try to attend to last-minute requests whenever possible.
