1. Why choose a direct transfer?

When leaving Conil with luggage and a fixed departure time on your ticket, the last thing you want is added uncertainty. Our service is based on absolute peace of mind:

Departure with margin

We recommend arriving at Santa Justa in advance, especially during peak hours. This way you can enter calmly, locate your platform, and avoid rushing with luggage.

From your door

We pick you up directly from your hotel or apartment. No hauling luggage through intermediate stops.

Fixed Rate

Knowledge of the exact cost before leaving. No unexpected supplements for traffic or luggage.

Punctuality

We plan the departure with the necessary margin so you reach your train without haste or stress.

Key fact: Direct buses to Seville usually drop you off at Prado de San Sebastián, forcing you to take another internal transport to reach Santa Justa station, adding time and effort.

2. Tips for a perfect departure

We recommend making your booking at least 24 hours in advance. This allows us to monitor possible road incidents and ensure a vehicle is at your door at the exact moment you need it.

Families

If you are travelling with children, indicate the need for child seats (SRI) when booking so everything is ready for your arrival.

XL Luggage

Ample trunks for large suitcases and strollers, without additional charges per item.

Safety Margin

We always suggest arriving at the station at least 30-40 minutes before the train departure to avoid rushing.

Check-in Ready

Have your Renfe tickets handy. We drop you off as close as possible to the access scanners.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the journey from Santa Justa to Conil take?
Approximately 1 hour and 40 minutes. We take the AP-4 and the A-48, with access by the SE-30 according to traffic, connecting directly the station with your destination.
What is the price of the taxi from Santa Justa?
We offer a fixed price of 24-256€ according to schedule and luggage. Tariff fixed without additional costs or management fees.
Where do they pick me up at the station?
We wait for you at the main exit of taxis in Santa Justa. We monitor your train to adjust pickup time if there are delays.
Is it better than other transport?
The taxi is the most comfortable and fastest option, available 24h for your arrival without waits.
Can intermediate stops be made?
Yes, we can make stops for rest, shopping or visiting points of interest. Just indicate it when booking and we will adjust the service.
