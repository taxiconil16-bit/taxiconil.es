Sunshine Tour Montenmedio: The Epicentre of World Equestrianism

Back to Blog

The Andalucía Sunshine Tour at Dehesa Montenmedio (Vejer de la Frontera) is not just a competition; it is the largest and most important equestrian circuit in Europe. With over 30 years of history, it has become the essential meeting point for the international showjumping elite.

Three Decades of Equestrian Excellence

Since its inception in 1994, the Sunshine Tour has transformed the province of Cádiz into the epicentre of the horse world during the winter months. What began as a modest event is now a 60-hectare "equestrian city" that attracts the best riders and horses from the world rankings.

Global Impact

More than 2,000 riders and 3,500 horses from 60 different countries participate.

9 Weeks of Elite Sport

The competition spans two months, being key for Olympic Games and World Championship qualifiers.

Top-Tier Facilities

It features 19 competition arenas (grass and sand), 960 permanent boxes, and world-class infrastructure.

Economic Relevance

It is one of the main tourism drivers of the Janda region, generating a vital economic impact for Conil, Vejer, and the surrounding area.
